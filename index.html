How can I improve my profit margin?
Great question! Here are the 3 fastest ways to boost your margin:

1. Raise price on top sellers by 8-10% — demand rarely drops for bestsellers.
2. Negotiate with suppliers — even a 5% COGS cut = massive margin jump.
3. Remove products with margin <8% — they're dragging your average down.
What's the best pricing strategy for my shop?
Value-Based Pricing is the most profitable for retailers. Price based on what the customer believes the product is worth — not just cost + markup. Combine with psychological pricing (₹499 vs ₹500) for maximum conversion.

✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is my business data safe? Where is it stored? +
100% safe. VENDRIXA IQ stores ALL your data directly in your browser's LocalStorage — it never leaves your device. There is no server, no cloud upload, and no third-party access. Your data is yours, period.
What file formats does the upload engine support? +
We support CSV (.csv), Excel 2007+ (.xlsx), and older Excel (.xls) formats. The AI auto-detects your column names even if they're different from our standard naming — and lets you manually map them if needed.
Do I need internet to use VENDRIXA IQ? +
You need internet only to load the page for the first time. Once loaded, the dashboard, AI chat, upload engine, and all modules work completely offline. Your data persists across sessions.
How accurate is the AI analyst? +
The AI is powered by a comprehensive seller knowledge base covering 25+ topics — pricing, GST, Amazon/Flipkart, supplier negotiation, seasonal strategy, and more. When you upload data, it gives personalised answers. Without data, it gives expert general advice based on Indian retail best practices.
Can I use it for my online store (Amazon/Flipkart/Meesho)? +
Yes! VENDRIXA IQ is designed for all types of sellers — offline kirana stores, wholesale distributors, and online marketplace sellers on Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Nykaa, and D2C websites. Just export your order report from any platform and upload it.
What happens to my data if I clear my browser? +
Clearing your browser storage will erase your VENDRIXA data since it's stored locally. We recommend downloading the Smart Report (CSV) regularly as a backup. A cloud sync feature is on our roadmap for future versions.
